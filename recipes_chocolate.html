Dark Chocolate

  • Rating :

  • Time : 3 hrs

  • Category : Anytime

  • Serves : 4

Ingredients:
1/2 cup melted butter
1 tbsp coco powder
2 tsp milk powder
1 tsp coriander powder
2 tbsp fresh curd
3 chopped green chillies
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 small onion chopped
1 small carrot chopped
freshly chopped coriander
Salt to taste
1 tsp oil
Water

Procedure:
1) Take a mixing bowl add all the ingredients in it (exept oil) and mix it well, add water if required.
2) Let the mixture rest for 15 mins.
3) Heat a aappe making pan add oil in each cavity and then slowly pour the batter in each cavity with the help of a spoon. Cover the pan with a lid and let it cook to golden brown.
4) After 5 mins invert the aappe using a fork or a knife. Cook for 2 mins and then remove them from the pan.
Tasty rava aappe are ready! Serve them with green chutney and enjoy!
