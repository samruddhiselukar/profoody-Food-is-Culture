Pizza

  • Rating :

  • Time : 45 mins

  • Category : Snacks

  • Serves : 2 pizzas

Ingredients:
1 cup wheat flour
1 cup curd
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
Butter
Bell peppers
Onion
Tomatoes
Paneer (optional)
Sweet corn (optional)
Red chillie sauce, Tomato sauce, Shezwan sauce
Italian seasoning, chillie flakes

Procedure:
1) Take a big bowl mix all the dry ingerdients together, then add mix curd and make a soft dough. Let the dough rest for 10 mins.
2) Till then take a big but deep pot, layer its bottom with salt (this gives oven like heating), cover it with a lid and put it on the cooktop on medium flame.
